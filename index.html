What Are Dental Implants?

Dental implants are titanium posts surgically placed into your jawbone to replace missing tooth roots. They provide a strong foundation for fixed or removable replacement teeth that look, feel, and function like natural teeth.

Benefits of Dental Implants:

  • Permanent solution for missing teeth
  • Prevents bone loss in the jaw
  • Restores ability to eat and speak comfortably
  • Looks and feels like natural teeth

Emergency Dentistry FAQs

Q: What counts as a dental emergency?

A: Severe tooth pain, knocked-out teeth, broken teeth with exposed nerves, abscesses, and uncontrolled bleeding are all dental emergencies requiring immediate care.

Q: Should I go to the ER for a dental emergency?

A: For most dental emergencies, call your dentist first. Go to the ER for jaw fractures, severe facial trauma, or uncontrolled bleeding.
