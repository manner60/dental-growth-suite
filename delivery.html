Getting Started Tips

1

Start with one assistant

Don't try to use all six at once. Pick one marketing need (social media or GBP posts) and master that assistant first.

2

Use the prompt starter sheet

Check your email for the prompt starter sheet with 10 ready-to-use prompts. Copy and paste these for best results.

3

Customize everything

The assistants give you strong starting points. Always review and customize content with your practice name, location, and unique voice.

4

Chain assistants together

Use Solis to plan your month, Zeta for social posts, Lex for a blog article, and Terra for GBP updates. They work best as a system.

5

Review for compliance

Always review AI-generated content for accuracy, compliance with dental regulations, and local relevance before publishing.
